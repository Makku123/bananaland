🃏 Poker Hand Guide
Hand Rankings (Best → Worst)
1 Royal Flush A K Q J 10 same suit
2 Straight Flush 5 in a row, same suit
3 Four of a Kind 4 cards same rank
4 Full House 3 of a kind + a pair
5 Flush 5 cards same suit
6 Straight 5 in a row, any suit
7 Three of a Kind 3 cards same rank
8 Two Pair 2 different pairs
9 One Pair 2 cards same rank
10 High Card Nothing — highest card wins
How It Works
You get 2 hole cards (private). Up to 5 community cards are dealt face-up in rounds. Make the best 5-card hand from your 2 + the community cards.
Rounds
Pre-Flop → 0 community cards
Flop → 3 community cards
Turn → 4 community cards
River → 5 community cards
Showdown → Cards revealed, best hand wins
Actions
Fold — Give up your hand (you lose your bets)
Check — Pass without betting (only if no one bet)
Call — Match the current bet
Raise — Increase the bet
All In — Bet everything you have
Tips for Beginners
• Pairs in your hand (e.g. two Kings) are strong starting hands
• High cards of the same suit (e.g. A♠ K♠) have flush potential
• Don't be afraid to fold weak hands — it saves 🍌
• If you have a pair + the board pairs, that's Two Pair or a Full House
• Bluffing (raising with a weak hand) can work — but be careful!
• SB (Small Blind) acts first after the flop; BB (Big Blind) has position advantage

🗺️ Variations & Rules

Preview how the board could look. Tiles are shuffled each game!

⚡ Quick Reference

🎯 Goal: Buy the ⭐ Magic Banana (7,777🍌) or 🍍 blow up an opponent
🎲 Movement: 2 dice + coin flip = 1–12 spaces
🗺️ Board: 52 tiles — 4 fixed corners, 48 shuffled
💰 Start: 1,000🍌 (configurable 100–4,000)
🏠 Farms: Won via auction, grow 🍌 at corners
🚫 No bankruptcy — you can't go below 0🍌
🎮 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Find and buy the ⭐ Magic Banana (costs 7,777🍌) to win instantly! It hides on a random tile each game — land on it with enough money and the game is yours.

🍍 Pineapple Bomb Mode: If enabled, you can also win by detonating a pineapple bomb under an opponent! See the Bombs section below.

In Teams mode (2v2), your team wins when either teammate buys the Magic Banana. A coin flip decides which team goes first (seats 1 & 3) and which goes second (seats 2 & 4). One random player from the second team gets a free scout pick for balance.

🎲 Turns & Dice

Each turn you roll 2 dice (1–6 each) plus a coin flip that adds 0 or −1. Your total move = die₁ + die₂ + coin, giving you 1 to 12 spaces clockwise around the 52-tile board.

🍌 Banana Piles: As you move, you automatically collect banana piles from your own farms along the path. When you land on an opponent's farm, you steal their banana pile!

🚫 No Bankruptcy

You cannot go below 0🍌. If a payment would take you negative, you simply pay what you can. Even at 0🍌 you stay in the game — no one is eliminated (except by ⭐ Super Banana or 🍍 Pineapple Bomb).

🗺️ The Board

🔀 Board Shuffling

Every game, the 48 non-corner tiles are randomly shuffled. Only the 4 GROW corners stay in fixed positions. The ⭐ Magic Banana hides in a new spot each game — no two games are alike!

At game start, all tiles are briefly revealed for 5 seconds before the board shuffles — memorize what you can!

🏠 Farm Groups

Farms are color-coded into 6 groups. Higher-yield farms are rarer and more valuable. Landing on an unowned farm triggers an auction — you don't buy directly.

Group Yield Count Rarity
🟡 Cavendish 50🍌 11 Common
🔵 Blue Java 100🍌 7 Common
🔴 Red Dacca 200🍌 5 Uncommon
🩷 Lady Finger 200🍌 5 Uncommon
� Gros Michel 360🍌 4 Rare
🟠 Goldfinger 500🍌 3 Legendary

Chain bonus: When you win an auction, any adjacent unowned farms of the same color are claimed for free! Position your bids strategically.

🌴 GROW Tiles (Corners)

The 4 corner tiles grow bananas on all your farms at once. Bananas go into each farm's banana pile, which you harvest when you pass over them on future turns.

Corner GROW % Position
🌴 GROW 100% 100% Bottom-left (Start)
🌴 GROW 25% 25% Top-left
🌴 GROW 50% 50% Top-right
🌴 GROW 75% 75% Bottom-right
📐 Growth Formula:
Growth = Yield × Set Multiplier × GROW% × (1 + Side Bonus)

Set Multiplier: Based on how many farms you own in that color group. Own 1 = 1× multiplier. Own 3 of 5 Red Dacca = each grows at 3×. Own all 5 = 5× each! Completing color sets is extremely powerful.

Side Bonus (based on which board edge the farm sits on):

  • Bottom row: +0%
  • Left column: +10%
  • Top row: +25%
  • Right column: +50%
📝 Example: You own 3 Red Dacca farms (yield 200🍌). One sits on the top row. You land on GROW 50%. Growth = 200 × 3 × 0.50 × (1 + 0.20) = 360🍌 added to that farm's banana pile.
🐒 Squatter Rule: If an opponent is sitting on your farm when it grows, they steal the growth instead of it going to the banana pile!

🏜️ Desert Tiles

8 desert tiles that cost 0🍌. They produce no yield and are excluded from GROW. Won through auctions like normal farms — but since they cost nothing, the winning bid is always 0. Useful for chain bonus positioning!

🍌 Tax Tiles

Landing on a tax tile takes a percentage of your bananas:

  • 🍌 Tax −10% — Lose 10% of your bananas (1 tile)
  • 🍌 Tax −15% — Lose 15% of your bananas (1 tile)

🌱 Easy Grow

A mini grow tile that triggers when you cross it or land on it. Grows bananas on all your farms at +10% rate. This tile shuffles with the rest of the board.

🌿 Vine Swing

Landing here lets you teleport to any farm you own. That farm's tile is then processed normally — you collect its banana pile, and it can trigger poker if an opponent is there. If you don't own any farms, nothing happens.

⭐ Super Banana

The legendary hidden ⭐ tile. Landing on it with 7,777🍌 or more means you buy it and win the game instantly!

Can't afford it? The tile is revealed to all players for 4.5 seconds, then it swaps to a random hidden tile elsewhere on the board. The tile left behind becomes a normal farm that gets auctioned.
No hidden tiles left? If you can't afford it and there are no hidden tiles to swap to — you are eliminated and the richest remaining player wins!
🔨 Auctions

🔨 Banana Bid (Standard Auction)

When you land on an unowned farm, an auction begins with 3 phases:

1 Lander bids — You name your opening price (visible to all)
2 Challengers respond — Other players can pass or place a blind bid (must be higher than yours)
3 Lander's final bid — If anyone challenged, you get one last blind bid to reclaim it

Highest bidder wins the farm and pays their bid. Ties go to the lander.

Broke? If you have 0🍌 and land on an unowned tile, the richest opponent claims it free. If everyone is broke, the lander gets it free.

🏷️ Simple Auction (Optional)

A faster auction variant for FFA games only. When enabled:

1 Lander names a price — You set the sale price for the farm
2 Opponents accept or decline — Each opponent chooses whether to buy it at your price
3 Tiebreaker — If multiple opponents accept, they enter a blind bid to decide who gets the right to buy

If no one accepts, the lander pays their own price and gets the farm. Great for speeding up games!

🤝 Teams Auction

In Teams mode, auctions are always 1v1: you vs. the closest opponent (ties broken by who has more 🍌). Same 3-phase flow as the standard auction, just with fewer bidders.

⚔️ Encounters

🃏 Poker (Texas Hold'em)

Triggered when two non-teammate players share the same tile and both have money. Play a heads-up hand of Texas Hold'em:

  • Landing player = Big Blind (up to 200🍌)
  • Other player = Small Blind (up to 100🍌)
  • Rounds: Preflop → Flop → Turn → River → Showdown
  • Standard poker hand rankings (pair, straight, flush, etc.)

Each round you can check, raise, or fold. Winner takes the pot. Tied hands split it evenly.

🐵 Monkey Poker (Optional)

A simplified poker variant — on by default. Replaces Texas Hold'em with a faster, luck-based card game:

  • Cards are numbered 1–10 (no suits)
  • Each player gets 1 card per round over 3 rounds
  • Rounds: Preflop → Flop → River → Showdown
  • At showdown, highest sum wins the pot
Why Monkey Poker? It's faster and more accessible than full Texas Hold'em. You still bluff and fold, but don't need to know poker hand rankings. Toggle it off in game settings for the real deal!

🍍 Pineapple Bombs (Optional)

When Bomb Mode is enabled, players can buy and plant hidden explosives on the board:

1 Buy a bomb for 5,000🍌 (one at a time)
2 Place it on any tile on the board (can't stack on existing bombs)
3 Wait... if an opponent lands on it — 💥 BOOM! You win!
⏳ Bombs expire! Each bomb lasts for 2 × (active players) turns. If no one triggers it in time, it fizzles out and your 5,000🍌 is gone. You can only hold one bomb at a time, and you can't trigger your own bomb.
🐾 Abilities & Tools

🐾 Pets

Choose a pet in the lobby. Each pet has a unique ability you can use after rolling on your turn:

Pet Ability Cooldown Limited
🦁 Strong Move forward 1 space (guaranteed) 20 rolls 1 use
🐆 Energy 50% chance to move forward 1 space 7 rolls 3 uses
🦄 Magic 50% chance to push an opponent forward 1 10 rolls 2 uses

Cooldown mode: Pet recharges after N rolls (counting all players' rolls). Limited mode: Fixed number of uses for the entire game — use them wisely!

🔭 Scouting

Optional pre-game ability. When enabled, each player picks one hidden tile to secretly reveal to themselves before the game begins. The tile stays hidden to everyone else until normally discovered. In Teams mode, one player scouts for both teammates.

⚙️ Game Modes & Settings

👥 Teams Mode (2v2)

4 players split into 2 teams. Either teammate buying the ⭐ Magic Banana wins it for the whole team.

  • No yield between teammates — landing on your teammate's farm is safe
  • Trade bananas with your teammate (50🍌 fee)
  • Swap farms with your teammate (100🍌 fee)
  • GROW counts teammate farms for set multiplier
  • Teammate banana piles are collected, not stolen
  • Auctions are 1v1 against closest opponent

These settings can be toggled when creating a game:
